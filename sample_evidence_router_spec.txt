NovaMesh AX6000 Router — Technical Specification Sheet

Wireless: Dual-band 802.11ax (Wi-Fi 6) radio, supporting simultaneous
2.4GHz and 5GHz operation with speeds up to 6000 Mbps combined. Backward
compatible with 802.11a/b/g/n/ac devices.

Security: Built-in Threat Protection powered by AI continuously monitors
network traffic and automatically blocks known malicious sites, malware,
and phishing attempts. Threat Protection is enabled by default and updates
its threat database automatically over the internet.

Mesh Networking: The NovaMesh AX6000 supports whole-home mesh Wi-Fi and can
be extended with up to 5 satellite mesh units, each communicating with the
main router over a dedicated wireless backhaul channel to extend coverage
without sacrificing speed.

Power and Ports: One 2.5GbE WAN port, four 1GbE LAN ports. Powered via the
included 12V/3A power adapter.
