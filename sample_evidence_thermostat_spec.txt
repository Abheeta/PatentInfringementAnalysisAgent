EcoTherm Smart Thermostat — Technical Specification Sheet

Sensing: The device uses an integrated NTC thermistor sensor to continuously
measure ambient room temperature with an accuracy of +/-0.5F. Temperature
readings are sampled every 30 seconds and displayed on the front LCD panel.

Connectivity: A 2.4GHz 802.11n Wi-Fi module connects the thermostat to the
home network. Once paired, the device syncs your schedule, target
temperature, and system mode settings with the EcoTherm cloud app so they
stay consistent across all your devices.

Display: The front-facing LCD shows the current room temperature, the
target setpoint, and the current system mode (Heat/Cool/Auto/Off). There is
no secondary sensor for humidity or air quality in this model.

Power: The thermostat is powered by the existing 24V HVAC control wiring;
no batteries are required for normal operation.
