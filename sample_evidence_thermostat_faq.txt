EcoTherm — Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is Smart Schedule?
A: Smart Schedule learns your routine over the first two weeks of use and
automatically adjusts your heating and cooling schedule to match, so you
don't have to program it manually. Most customers see it start making
useful suggestions within the first week.

Q: Does Smart Schedule know when I'm home?
A: Smart Schedule adapts based on how you interact with the thermostat over
time — we don't publish the internal details of how it decides when to
adjust the schedule.

Q: Can EcoTherm tell me the humidity in my house?
A: No. EcoTherm is a temperature-only thermostat and does not include a
humidity sensor. If you need humidity monitoring, pair it with a separate
EcoTherm Room Sensor accessory (sold separately).

Q: Does EcoTherm work without Wi-Fi?
A: Yes, basic heating/cooling control works locally. Wi-Fi is required for
remote access, schedule syncing, and app notifications.
